A Place to Stay

In July 2013 I received a call from a friend of mine who asked me if I was interested to do a charity job by being a part of publishing a book by taking some pictures of Kahrizak Charity Foundation.

Kahrizak Charity Foundation is a private, non-governmental, non-profit, charitable organization; where physically handicapped or elderly individuals with no financial resources are cared for, free of charge. 500 male and 1100 female patients reside in 22 separate units. About two thirds of the patients are elderly and one third are physically disabled.

The concept of the book was to show the hope and a good face of the Institute and collect money for it by selling the books outside the country to make it more advanced for the patients.

It took me 2 months to finish this project. I took over 200 shots in black and white and selected a few as a series.
