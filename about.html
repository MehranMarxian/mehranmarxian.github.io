About Mehran Ahmadi

Mehran Ahmadi is an Iranian Freelance Artist / Photographer who is currently living and wandering in Tehran.

I am a designer, artist, and photographer with a passion for visual storytelling. My work spans across photography, computer graphics, and advanced AI techniques, where I constantly explore surrealism through art. I believe in crafting experiences that resonate emotionally with viewers, combining my technical skills with an artistic vision that pushes creative boundaries.
