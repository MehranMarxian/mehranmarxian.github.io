Image Processing


Image Processing based on Chaos Theory

Chaos theory is a branch of mathematics focusing on the behavior of dynamical systems that are highly sensitive to initial conditions. Within chaotic systems, patterns, self-similarity, and self-organization emerge.

I achieved interesting results by linking chaos theory attributes to Color Gain, Color Offset, HSV, and UV Coordinates to control certain aspects of my photographs.


A double-rod pendulum animation illustrating chaotic behavior. Starting from slightly different initial conditions results in completely different trajectories, showcasing chaotic solutions.
